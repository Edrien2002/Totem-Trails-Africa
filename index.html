Our Strategic Pillars

Four mutually reinforcing pillars that ensure our work is developmental and sustainable

Cultural Integrity

Respecting and documenting over 1,000 totems across Uganda's 60+ tribes, using elders as primary knowledge custodians.

Scientific Excellence

Aligning cultural taboos with modern conservation biology to protect species like the Pangolin and Crested Crane.

Technological Innovation

Using gamification, AR and AI to create personalized storylines about nature for Africa's youth.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Creating a "Digital Employment Pipeline" for youth as digital naturalists, eco-mappers, and cultural storytellers.
