Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our work and partnerships

How can my organization partner with Totem Trails Africa?

We welcome partnerships with conservation organizations, tech companies, academic institutions, and community groups. Please email totemtrailsafrica256@gmail.com with details about your organization and proposed collaboration ideas.

Do you offer volunteer or internship opportunities?

Yes! We offer both local and remote volunteer/internship positions in conservation, technology, community engagement, and research. Positions are announced on our website and social media channels.

How can I support your work financially?

We accept donations through various channels including direct bank transfers, mobile money, and online platforms. You can also sponsor specific projects or adopt a totem. Contact totemtrailsafrica256@gmail.com for more information.

Can communities outside Uganda participate in your programs?

Absolutely! While we're based in Uganda, our digital platforms and community engagement models are designed to work across Africa. We're actively expanding to other African countries and welcome inquiries from communities interested in our approach.

How do you ensure cultural sensitivity in your work?

We work directly with community elders and cultural custodians in all our projects. Our team includes cultural anthropologists and community engagement specialists who ensure our work respects and honors local traditions and knowledge systems.
