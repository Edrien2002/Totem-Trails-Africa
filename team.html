The Founders

Totem Trails Africa was founded in 2025 by visionary leaders passionate about blending African heritage with modern technology.

Mutebi brings extensive experience in conservation biology and a deep understanding of Uganda's ecological systems. His passion lies in bridging traditional ecological knowledge with modern scientific approaches.

Our Multi-Disciplinary Teams

Four specialized teams working together to achieve our mission

Identity Science Innovators

Experts in documenting and digitizing indigenous knowledge systems. This team works directly with community elders to preserve and translate traditional wisdom into digital formats.

8 Team Members 60+ Tribes Documented

Conservation Biologists

Scientists ensuring that all cultural actions lead to measurable biodiversity recovery. They monitor species populations and ecosystem health across our project areas.

12 Team Members 50+ Species Protected

Technology Architects

Developers specializing in AR, AI, and gamification to build the world-class TTA platform. They transform cultural concepts into engaging digital experiences.

15 Team Members 4 Platforms Developed

Community Engagement Specialists

Experts in clan onboarding and youth outreach. They build relationships with communities and ensure our work is culturally appropriate and community-driven.

10 Team Members 1000+ Clans Engaged
